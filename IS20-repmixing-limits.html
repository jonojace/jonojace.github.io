Testing the Limits of Representation Mixing for Pronunciation Correction in End-to-End Speech Synthesis

by Jason Fong, Jason Taylor, Simon King

'In-LJ' Test Set:

Words that occur in LJ Speech but were never phonemicised during training, and are mispronounced by the baseline grapheme-only model: despite being in the training data, they are surprisingly still mispronounced when represented as graphemes. This set investigates which models offer effective phone correction for this category of unexpected mispronunciations.
